Biography

A life given to the Word — told in full.

From a childhood decision at age twelve to a calling that now spans medicine, theology, and pastoral leadership — the story of Dr. Abraham Achimugu.

Dr. Abraham Achimugu Lead Pastor, Eden Garden Foursquare Church
Chapter 01 — Early Life & Salvation

A decision made
at twelve.

Dr. Abraham Achimugu was born on the 23rd of November 1988, the 4th child of Rev. Clement and Pst. Josephine Achimugu. Raised in a home already given to ministry, he accepted Jesus Christ at the age of twelve — and preached his first sermon that very same year.

It was an early beginning, but not an accidental one. The conviction that took root in that season would go on to shape every decision that followed — where he studied, how he led, and the life he would eventually build.

Chapter 02 Mentorship & Formation

Shaped by those who came before.

His first and most formative disciple-maker was his own father, who mentored him from the very beginning of his walk with God. As his ministry matured, he came under the mentorship of Dr. Arome Osayi, whose guidance continues to shape his approach to ministry today.

Beyond direct mentorship, his life and teaching have been sharpened from a distance by men like Bishop Dag Heward-Mills and Derek Prince — voices whose writing and example helped form his conviction that sound doctrine and a disciplined life are not optional extras, but the foundation of lasting ministry.

Chapter 03 Education & Calling

A man given to knowledge.

Dr. Achimugu holds a Bachelor's degree in Physiology, a Bachelor's degree in Theology, a Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS), and a Master's in Public Health. He currently serves as a Specialist in Obstetrics and Gynaecology, with a future sub-specialization planned in fertility medicine.

For him, the pursuit of medical and theological knowledge has never been two separate callings running side by side — it is one calling, expressed in two languages: the care of the body, and the care of the soul.

"Science did not distance me from God. It gave me another language to describe what I already believed about Him." Dr. Abraham Achimugu
Chapter 04 Ministry Journey

From campus leadership to the pulpit.

Dr. Achimugu began active ministry in 2004 and has held several leadership positions since his days on campus. In 2013, he served as Zonal Coordinator of the Nigeria Christian Corpers' Fellowship (NCCF), Ikara Zone — an early proving ground for the leadership he would go on to carry.

That journey has led him to his current assignment as the Lead Pastor of Eden Garden Foursquare Church, Ilorin, where he continues the work of teaching, discipling, and raising leaders within the local church.

Chapter 05 Books & Teaching

Words that outlast a sermon.

Alongside his pulpit ministry, Dr. Achimugu is the author of several books exploring obedience, identity, and the disciplined life of a minister — resources now being used well beyond the walls of any one church.

Chapter 06 Family & Present Assignment

A home that mirrors the message.

Dr. Achimugu is married to Pst. Damilola Achimugu, a Biomedical Engineer, and their union is blessed with a daughter, Havvah. Their home, much like his ministry, holds faith and expertise together without tension — each strengthening the other.

Today, Dr. Achimugu continues to serve as Lead Pastor of Eden Garden Foursquare Church, Ilorin, while carrying the wider work of DAAM — teaching, writing, and raising leaders across the Gospel Outreaches, Medical, Academic, Hospitality, and Publications arms of the ministry.

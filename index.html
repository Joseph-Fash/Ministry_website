About Dr. Abraham Achimugu

A life given
to the Word.

Born on the 23rd of November 1988, the 4th child of Rev. Clement and Pst. Josephine Achimugu, Dr. Abraham Achimugu accepted Christ at age 12 and preached his first sermon that same year. Discipled by his father and mentored by Dr. Arome Osayi, his life and ministry have also been shaped by men like Bishop Dag Heward-Mills and Derek Prince.

A man given to knowledge, he holds degrees in Physiology, Theology, Medicine and Surgery, and Public Health, and serves as a Specialist in Obstetrics and Gynaecology. He is currently the Lead Pastor of Eden Garden Foursquare Church, Ilorin — married to Pst. Damilola Achimugu, a Biomedical Engineer, and they are blessed with a daughter, Havvah.
